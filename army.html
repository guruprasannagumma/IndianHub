About Indian Army

The Indian Army is the land-based branch of the armed forces of India. It is one of the largest and strongest armies in the world, responsible for protecting the country from external threats and maintaining peace and security.

Key Responsibilities

Border Security

Protecting India's borders from external threats.

Disaster Relief

Helping citizens during floods, earthquakes, and emergencies.

Peace Keeping

Participating in global peace missions.

Achievements

Kargil Victory

Successful operation to protect India's territory.

UN Missions

India is one of the largest contributors to UN peacekeeping.

World's Largest Army

One of the strongest military forces globally.
